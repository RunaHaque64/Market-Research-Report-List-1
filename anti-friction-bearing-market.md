<p><p>The anti-friction bearing market comprises three primary types: ball bearings, roller bearings, and other bearings. Ball bearings utilize spherical elements to reduce friction, ideal for high-speed applications. Roller bearings feature cylindrical rollers, which can support heavier loads and are suitable for varied radial and axial loads. Other types include needle bearings, which have a smaller cross-section and provide high load capacity, and magnetic bearings that minimize friction using magnetic fields. Together, these variations cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
