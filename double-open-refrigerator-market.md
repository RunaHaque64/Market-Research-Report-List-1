<p><p>The double-open refrigerator market features three main types: direct-cooled, air-cooled, and mixed refrigeration. Direct-cooled systems utilize refrigerant to directly cool the interior, ensuring efficient temperature maintenance. Air-cooled units rely on ambient air circulation for cooling, offering energy efficiency and lower maintenance. Mixed refrigeration combines both methods, providing flexibility and enhanced cooling performance. Each type caters to different consumer needs, applications, and energy requirements, making them suitable for various retail and commercial environments.</p></p>
