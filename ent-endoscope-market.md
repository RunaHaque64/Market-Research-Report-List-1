<p><p>The ENT endoscope market is primarily categorized into rigid and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes feature a solid structure, providing clear imaging for procedures like sinus surgery and laryngeal examinations. They are ideal for specific angles and offer superior visualization. In contrast, flexible endoscopes are more adaptable, allowing for navigation through intricate anatomical pathways. They are commonly used for diagnostics and therapeutic procedures in the ear, nose, and throat regions, enhancing accessibility and patient comfort during examinations.</p></p>
