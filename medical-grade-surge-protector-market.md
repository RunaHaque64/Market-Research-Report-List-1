<p><p>The medical grade surge protector market is segmented into three main types: Voltage Switch Type, Pressure Limiting Type, and Combination Type. Voltage Switch Type protects equipment by diverting excess voltage, while Pressure Limiting Type safeguards against surges by controlling pressure levels in medical devices. Combination Type integrates features of both, offering comprehensive protection by managing voltage and pressure simultaneously. These types ensure the safety and reliability of medical equipment, crucial for maintaining patient care and preventing costly damage.</p></p>
