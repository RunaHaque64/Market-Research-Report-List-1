<p><p>The medical micro surgical drill market features three primary types: pneumatic drills, electric drills, and battery-powered drills. Electric drills are powered by electricity, providing consistent power and speed, making them suitable for various surgical applications. Battery-powered drills enhance mobility and convenience, eliminating the need for electrical outlets, which is beneficial in remote or complex surgical environments. Each type serves unique operational needs in micro surgery.</p></p>
