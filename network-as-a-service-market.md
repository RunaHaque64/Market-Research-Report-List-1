<p><p>Network as a Service (NaaS) encompasses various models, including LAN-as-a-Service (LANaaS) and WAN-as-a-Service (WANaaS). LANaaS offers virtualized local area networks, enabling businesses to scale their connectivity easily without investing in physical infrastructure. Conversely, WANaaS provides wide area network services, facilitating seamless communication across geographically dispersed locations. Both models allow organizations to achieve flexibility, reduce capital expenditures, and enhance operational efficiency by leveraging cloud solutions for networking needs, ultimately supporting their digital transformation.</p></p>
