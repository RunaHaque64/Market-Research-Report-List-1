<p><p>The vehicle DPF retrofit market consists of two primary types: Cordierite DPF and Silicon Carbide (SiC) DPF. Cordierite DPFs are lightweight and have good thermal shock resistance, making them ideal for light-duty applications. In contrast, SiC DPFs offer superior filtration efficiency and thermal durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles operating in demanding conditions. Both types are essential for meeting emissions regulations and improving vehicle performance, catering to various industry needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
