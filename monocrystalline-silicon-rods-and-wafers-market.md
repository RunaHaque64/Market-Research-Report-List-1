<p><p>Monocrystalline silicon rods and wafers are essential components in the semiconductor and solar industries. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Czochralski (CZ) monocrystalline and Float Zone (FZ) monocrystalline. CZ monocrystalline silicon is produced using the Czochralski process, which is cost-effective and widely used for solar cells and electronics. In contrast, FZ monocrystalline silicon is created through a more refined process that results in higher purity and superior electronic properties, making it preferred for high-performance applications in advanced semiconductors.</p></p>
