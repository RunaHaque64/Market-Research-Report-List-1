<p><p>The Fire Hazard Assessment market comprises various specialized methodologies to evaluate fire risks in environments. Computer-Based Fire Modeling simulates fire behavior, while Structural Response Modeling assesses how structures respond to fire exposure. Modeling of Fire Protection System Response evaluates the effectiveness of safety systems. Explosion Hazard Assessment focuses on blast risks, while Preliminary Hazard Assessment (PHA) identifies initial hazards. Hazard and Operability Studies (HAZOP) analyze process safety, and Failure Modes and Effects Analysis (FMEA) evaluates potential failure impacts. Event Tree Analysis maps possible accident sequences, aiding risk management.</p></p>
