<p><p>The fluoropolymer coatings market includes various types like PTFE, PVDF, FEP, and ETFE, each offering unique properties. PTFE coatings are known for their excellent non-stick and chemical resistance, ideal for cookware and industrial applications. PVDF coatings provide superior weather resistance and durability, commonly used in architectural finishes. FEP coatings exhibit excellent clarity and chemical resistance, suitable for electronic components. ETFE coatings are lightweight and have high corrosion resistance, making them popular in aerospace and industrial sectors.</p></p>
