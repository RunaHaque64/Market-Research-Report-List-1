<p><strong>The Hip Scopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pincer Type</li><li>Cam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hip scopes are categorized based on their market types: Pincer Type and Cam Type. Pincer type hip scopes are designed to address femoroacetabular impingement where a labral tear may occur, emphasizing the need for stabilization during movement. In contrast, Cam type hip scopes focus on the bony deformity at the femoral head, which can lead to joint damage over time. Both types facilitate minimally invasive surgeries aimed at restoring hip function and alleviating pain, catering to various joint pathologies.</p></p>
