Wheel spoke sensors utilize the wheel's structural components to gauge load, while torque transducers measure rotational force, providing insights into vehicle dynamics. Hole sensors are embedded in structural elements, detecting changes in capacitance due to varying loads. S-sensors are designed for applications requiring precise load measurement along specific vehicle dimensions, enhancing safety and performance by ensuring optimal weight distribution across the vehicle.</p></p>
