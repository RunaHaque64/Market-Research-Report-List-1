High purity ITO coatings, containing more than 90% of indium oxide, are prized for their superior electrical conductivity and optical transparency, making them ideal for high-end applications like touchscreens and OLED displays. Low purity ITO, with a lower indium content, is cost-effective and suitable for less critical applications. Both types serve distinct market needs across various industries, including electronics and optics.</p></p>
