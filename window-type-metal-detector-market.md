<p><p>The window-type metal detector market features two primary types: balanced coil systems and ferrous-in-foil systems. Balanced coil systems utilize two coils that create a balanced electromagnetic field, detecting metallic objects through changes in this balance. Ferrous-in-foil systems, on the other hand, consist of sensors designed to identify metal contaminants within foil packaging, focusing specifically on ferrous metals. Both systems are widely used in food processing and packaging industries to ensure product safety and maintain quality standards.</p></p>
