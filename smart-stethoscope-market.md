<p><p>The smart stethoscope market is primarily categorized into two types: wireless stethoscopes and wired stethoscopes. Wireless stethoscopes utilize Bluetooth or other wireless technologies to transmit audio directly to mobile devices or computers, enhancing mobility and convenience for healthcare professionals. In contrast, wired stethoscopes connect directly to devices via cables, providing reliable audio quality with minimal latency. Both types offer advanced features such as digital sound amplification, data recording, and real-time sharing, improving diagnostic capabilities and patient care.</p></p>
