<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) apparatus market is categorized into three main types based on magnetic field strength. Low-Field MRI machines, operating between 0.23-0.3T, are cost-effective and suitable for general imaging but offer lower resolution. High-Field MRI systems, ranging from 1.5 to 3.0T, provide improved clarity and detail, making them ideal for advanced diagnostic applications. Ultra-High Field MRI, surpassing 7.0T, delivers exceptional imaging capabilities, particularly for research purposes, but is more expensive and less commonly used in routine clinical settings.</p></p>
