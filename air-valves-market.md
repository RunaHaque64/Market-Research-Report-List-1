<p><strong>The Air Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Release Valves</li><li>Air/Vacuum Valves</li><li>Combination Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air valves market consists of three primary types: Air-Release Valves, which allow trapped air to escape from pipelines, improving flow efficiency; Air/Vacuum Valves, designed to intake air during negative pressure conditions and release air when needed, preventing collapses in systems; and Combination Valves, which integrate the functions of both Air-Release and Air/Vacuum Valves, providing comprehensive solutions for pipeline maintenance. These valves are essential for maintaining optimal performance and preventing damage in fluid transportation systems.</p></p>
