<p><ul><li>Absolute Auction</li><li>Minimum Bid Auction</li><li>Reserve Auction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auction services market encompasses various types of bidding processes including absolute auctions, minimum bid auctions, and reserve auctions. In an absolute auction, items are sold to the highest bidder without any minimum price requirements, ensuring a sale regardless of the offer. Minimum bid auctions require bidders to meet a set threshold before participating, while reserve auctions allow sellers to establish a hidden minimum price, ensuring goods are not sold below this predetermined amount. Each method caters to different seller needs and buyer strategies.</p></p>
