<p><p>The Automatic Voltage Regulator (AVR) market consists of several types, including linear regulators, shunt voltage regulators, and others. Linear regulators maintain a constant output voltage by adjusting the resistance in response to changes in load, offering low noise and simplicity. Shunt voltage regulators function by diverting excess current to ground, thus stabilizing voltage levels effectively for lower power applications. Other types include switching regulators and digital voltage controllers, catering to diverse electrical needs across various industries.</p></p>
