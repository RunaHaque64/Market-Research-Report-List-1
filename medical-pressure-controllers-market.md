<p><p>The medical pressure controllers market comprises two main types: tube type and cuff type. Tube type controllers utilize flexible tubing to regulate and monitor pressure in various medical devices, offering precision in fluid delivery. Cuff type controllers, commonly used in blood pressure monitoring, consist of an inflatable cuff that applies pressure to the limb, enabling easy measurement of arterial pressure. Both types are essential for ensuring accurate patient monitoring and treatment in clinical settings, catering to diverse healthcare needs.</p></p>
