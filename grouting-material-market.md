<p><p>The grouting material market primarily features two main types: urethane-based and epoxy-based grouts. Urethane-based grouts are known for their flexibility, excellent adhesion, and resistance to water and chemical infiltration, making them ideal for applications in areas prone to movement or fluctuations in moisture levels. In contrast, epoxy-based grouts offer superior strength, durability, and chemical resistance, making them well-suited for industrial environments and heavy-duty applications. Both types serve distinct needs, catering to different sectors in construction and maintenance.</p></p>
