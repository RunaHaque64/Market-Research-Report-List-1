<p><strong>The Impact Crusher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Impact Crusher</li><li>Vertical Impact Crusher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impact crusher market can be divided into horizontal impact crushers and vertical impact crushers. Horizontal impact crushers are designed to process materials horizontally, utilizing a horizontal rotor to deliver high energy to the feed material, making them ideal for primary and secondary crushing. In contrast, vertical impact crushers, or vertical shaft impactors, utilize a vertical rotor to achieve high-speed impacts, suitable for producing finely crushed aggregates and sand. Each type caters to specific industry needs and material processing requirements.</p></p>
