<p><p>The Fiber Optic Cable Assembly market features two primary types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cables. Single-Mode Fiber is designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path, resulting in minimal signal loss and higher bandwidth over extensive ranges. In contrast, Multi-Mode Fiber is suited for shorter distances, allowing multiple light paths, which increases capacity but can lead to signal degradation over longer stretches. Both types are essential for various applications, including telecommunications, data networks, and broadcasting.</p></p>
