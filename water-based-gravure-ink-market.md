<p><p>The water-based gravure ink market is primarily segmented into two types: Photo Gravure Ink and Intaglio Gravure Ink. Photo Gravure Ink is designed for high-resolution printing applications, offering superior color vibrancy and detail, often used in packaging and labeling. Intaglio Gravure Ink, on the other hand, is specifically formulated for the intaglio printing process, providing excellent adhesion and durability on various substrates. Both types cater to environmentally friendly printing solutions, leveraging water as a base for reduced solvent emissions.</p></p>
