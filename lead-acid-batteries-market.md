<p><strong>The Lead-Acid Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead-acid battery market is primarily categorized into two types: Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid (VRLA) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries, sealed and maintenance-free, provide improved performance and are ideal for applications requiring compact, efficient power, such as in telecommunications and renewable energy systems. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, involve liquid electrolyte and need regular maintenance, making them suitable for applications like automotive and industrial use where cost-efficiency and reliability are essential in high-capacity settings.</p></p>
