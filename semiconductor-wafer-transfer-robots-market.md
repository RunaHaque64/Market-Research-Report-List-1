<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Transfer Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Manipulator</li><li>Vacuum Manipulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer transfer robots market is categorized into two main types: atmospheric manipulators and vacuum manipulators. Atmospheric manipulators operate in normal atmospheric conditions, effectively transferring wafers between processing tools while maintaining cleanliness and efficiency. In contrast, vacuum manipulators function in controlled vacuum environments, minimizing contamination and allowing for precise handling of sensitive wafers. Both types are crucial for optimizing semiconductor fabrication processes, enhancing yield, and ensuring the integrity of the delicate materials used in chip production.</p></p>
