<p><strong>The Plastic Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Single-screw</li><li>Double-screw</li><li>Multi-screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic extruder market comprises several types, each catering to specific processing needs. Plunger extruders use a piston mechanism to push material through a die, ideal for high-viscosity materials. Single-screw extruders are the most common, featuring a single screw for continuous material feeding and mixing. Double-screw extruders, either co-rotating or counter-rotating, provide enhanced mixing and are suitable for a wider range of materials. Multi-screw extruders utilize multiple screws for high output and precise control, ideal for complex applications.</p></p>
