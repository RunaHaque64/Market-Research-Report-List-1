<p><strong>The Facial Motion Capture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Optical</li><li>Electromagnetic (magnetic)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Facial motion capture technology is categorized into three main types: mechanical, optical, and electromagnetic. Mechanical systems use physical markers and sensors attached to the face to track movements, offering high precision. Optical systems rely on cameras and software to capture facial expressions through visual data, enabling a markerless approach. Electromagnetic systems utilize magnetic fields and sensors to track facial movements, providing flexibility but requiring careful calibration. Each type serves various applications in animation, gaming, and virtual reality, ensuring accurate representation of facial expressions.</p></p>
