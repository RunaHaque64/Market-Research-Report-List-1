<p><strong>The Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) Therapeutics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The GERD therapeutics market includes several key medication types. Antacids neutralize stomach acid for quick relief of symptoms. Pro-kinetic agents improve gastric emptying and reduce reflux episodes. H2 receptor blockers decrease acid production by blocking histamine receptors, providing longer relief. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) are the most effective, inhibiting acid production at the source and offering sustained relief from GERD symptoms. Together, these therapies cater to varying severity levels and patient needs in managing gastroesophageal reflux disease.</p></p>
