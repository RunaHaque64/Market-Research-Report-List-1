<p><strong>The Enteral Feeding Bags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump Type</li><li>Gravity Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enteral feeding bags are essential for delivering nutrition directly into the gastrointestinal tract. The market is primarily segmented into two types: pump type and gravity type. Pump type bags utilize a mechanical pump to control the flow rate of the feeding formula, ensuring precise delivery and reducing the risk of aspiration. In contrast, gravity type bags rely on gravity to facilitate the flow, making them simpler but potentially less controlled. Both types cater to varied patient needs and caregiver preferences in clinical settings.</p></p>
