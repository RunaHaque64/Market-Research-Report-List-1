<p><p>The wheelchair market is primarily divided into two main types: electric wheelchairs and manual wheelchairs. Electric wheelchairs are powered by batteries, providing enhanced mobility for users with limited physical strength or those needing longer-distance travel. They often come with advanced features, including joystick controls and customizable seating. In contrast, manual wheelchairs require physical effort to operate, making them more lightweight and portable, ideal for short distances or users with upper body strength. Each type meets specific user needs and preferences.</p></p>
