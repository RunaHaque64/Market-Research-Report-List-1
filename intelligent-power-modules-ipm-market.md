<p><strong>The Intelligent Power Modules IPM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IGBT-IPM</li><li>MOSFET-IPM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent Power Module (IPM) market can be segmented into two primary types: IGBT-IPM and MOSFET-IPM. IGBT-IPM combines insulated gate bipolar transistors with advanced control circuitry, offering high voltage and current handling capabilities, making it ideal for applications like motor drives and renewable energy systems. Conversely, MOSFET-IPM utilizes metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors for faster switching characteristics and lower losses, suitable for applications requiring high efficiency, such as power supplies and electric vehicles. Both types enhance performance and reliability in power electronics.</p></p>
