<p><p>The gas-sensitive semiconductor market is categorized into two main types: poisonous gas and general gas sensors. General gas sensors encompass a broader range of gases, including those used in environmental monitoring and air quality assessment, such as methane and volatile organic compounds. Both types are essential for enhancing public safety, environmental protection, and industrial process control.</p></p>
