<p><p>The cognitive assessment and training market comprises three main types: Pen & Paper Tests, Online Tests, and Biometric Assessments. Online Tests utilize digital platforms for more efficient and scalable assessments, often providing instant feedback and analytics. Biometric Assessments incorporate physiological measurements, such as eye movement or brainwaves, to gauge cognitive performance, offering a more nuanced understanding of mental processes and capabilities.</p></p>
