<p><p>The light-duty electric trucks market is primarily categorized into two types: pure electric power and hybrid power. Pure electric power trucks are fully electric, relying solely on battery power for propulsion, offering zero emissions and lower operating costs. In contrast, hybrid power trucks utilize both an electric motor and an internal combustion engine, combining the benefits of electric efficiency with traditional fuel range. This dual approach provides flexibility in usage and reduces reliance on charging infrastructure, appealing to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
