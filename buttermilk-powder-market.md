<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Buttermilk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dried Buttermilk Powder</li><li>Atmospheric Roller Dried Buttermilk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buttermilk powder market features two main types: spray dried buttermilk powder and atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder. Spray dried buttermilk powder is produced by rapidly evaporating moisture from liquid buttermilk, resulting in a fine powder with excellent solubility and shelf stability. In contrast, atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder involves spreading liquid buttermilk onto heated rollers, creating flakes that are then ground into powder. Both types serve various applications in the food industry, enhancing flavor, texture, and nutritional value in products.</p></p>
