<p><p>The Metal Release Agent market is categorized into two main types: permanent and semi-permanent agents. Permanent release agents create a lasting barrier on the mold surface, allowing multiple uses without reapplication, ideal for high-production environments. In contrast, semi-permanent release agents require occasional reapplication, providing flexibility and ease of use, particularly for lower production volumes or intricate molds. These agents ensure smooth separation of metal components from molds while minimizing damage and maintaining surface quality.</p></p>
